\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
A system that acts on entities. Generally, this will act on a subset of entities using \mbox{\hyperlink{classECS_1_1World_ac1060983911a221c120ef1bf370a6eee}{World\+::each()}}.

Systems often will respond to events by subclassing \mbox{\hyperlink{classECS_1_1EventSubscriber}{Event\+Subscriber}}. You may use \mbox{\hyperlink{classECS_1_1EntitySystem_af3b321db010c23da73fa270b8c7fb865}{configure()}} to subscribe to events, but remember to unsubscribe in \mbox{\hyperlink{classECS_1_1EntitySystem_afa595ef472522135f8736940c98d0aa0}{unconfigure()}}.
